Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

One-Time Payments

All Speak to Heaven purchases are one-time payments. We do not charge recurring subscriptions.

If you purchase access for a fixed period, your access will automatically end when that period expires. You do not need to cancel anything.

If you purchase Lifetime Access, your access remains active indefinitely. Since this is a lifetime purchase, there is no recurring billing and no cancellation is required.

Cancellation Policy

Because all payments are one-time payments, there are no automatic renewals. Once your selected access period ends, your paid access will simply expire unless you choose to make another purchase.

Refund Policy

Refunds may be considered when there is a valid and verifiable reason. Examples may include:

  • Your card or payment method was used without your permission.
  • You were charged incorrectly because of a technical error.
  • You made a duplicate payment by mistake.
  • You purchased the wrong access option and contacted us promptly.
  • You were unable to access the service because of a verified technical issue on our side.
  • There was a clear billing mistake that can be confirmed.

Refund requests are reviewed individually. We may ask for information needed to verify the payment, account, and reason for the request.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us through our contact page: Contact Speak to Heaven.

Please include your name, email address used for the purchase, date of payment, and the reason for your refund request.

Processing Time

If your refund is approved, it will be returned to the original payment method where possible. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.
