1. Digital Service Description

Speak To Heaven is a "Software as a Service" (SaaS) digital platform operated by RIDGWELL SERVICES LIMITED. The platform provides a simulated interface for users to interact with Artificial Intelligence (AI) representations of religious figures. This is a purely digital entertainment and educational product.

2. AI Limitations & Historical Disclaimer

The AI "Voices" are computer-generated approximations based on historical data. They do not possess actual memories or consciousness. The AI may generate factually incorrect historical data (hallucinations). The service should not be used as a primary source for academic research.

3. Payment Terms & Cardholder Disclosure

All payments are processed as one-time transactions. To ensure security, all transactions are subject to 3D Secure (3DS) authentication. Charges will appear on your bank statement as: STHCHARGE. All listed prices are in British Pounds (£).

4. Membership Plans & Access

The Company offers three distinct access tiers for the digital archive:

  • Single Figure Access (£29.95): Provides access to one (1) specific religious figure for a duration of one (1) month.
  • Full Archive Monthly (£35.95): Provides access to all religious figures within the platform for a duration of one (1) month.
  • Full Archive Quarterly (£49.95): Provides access to all religious figures within the platform for a duration of three (3) months.

5. Access Delivery

Access to the historical simulations is delivered digitally and immediately upon successful payment verification. No physical shipping is required.
